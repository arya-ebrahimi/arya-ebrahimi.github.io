I am an undergraduate student of Computer Engineering at the Ferdowsi University of Mashhad. Cognition and the process of learning in living beings have always sparked my curiosity about how to develop rational agents that can thrive in an environment. Throughout my undergraduate studies, this curiosity led me to explore research fields that might address this question, guiding me toward reinforcement learning. More precisely, I am interested in developing continual reinforcement learning agents that can leverage their previous knowledge and continually adapt to new challenges. 

Feel free to explore my blog and GitHub to see some of my projects, and I'll be happy if you reach out to collaborate.
